Privacy Policy for My Game Journal

Thank you for using My Game Journal. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

My Game Journal does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

The app does not require you to:

  • Create an account
  • Sign in
  • Provide your name or email address
  • Share your location

Your Data

Your game library, notes, play sessions, ratings, and settings are stored locally on your device.

The app does not upload your data to any server.

Third-Party Services

My Game Journal does not use:

  • Advertising services
  • Analytics services
  • Tracking technologies
  • Cloud storage services

Children's Privacy

My Game Journal does not knowingly collect personal information from users of any age.
